About Us

SYRG is a community-focused running group centered around Switchyard Park and the surrounding trails. We welcome runners of all paces and distances, with a mix of social runs, trail outings, and casual gatherings.

Our weekly schedule is designed to offer flexibility and consistency, so everyone can join at their comfort level and enjoy the local routes.
